Someone you never knew.

And someone you'll never remember.

You may know many more people today than the average person knew in the year 1800 or 1900. Still, most people alive in your own lifetime will be unknown to you. You won't know them; you won't remember them. They won't know you. They won't remember you.

This need not be a terrible thought. Use it when you feel overwhelmed, as a reminder of what really matters.
